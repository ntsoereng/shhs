At Sacred Heart, our departments are divided into different categories to achieve our common educational goals, listed below.

Languages Department

English Language

We consider English language playing an essential role in our school lives as it helps in communication. We put emphasis on the use of English as an International language given that English is the main language for studying any subject not only during the school years at Sacred Heart High School but also preparing our learners for further education all over the world. Therefore, English is important for students as it broadens their minds, develops emotional skills, improve the quality of life by providing job opportunities.

Sesotho Language

Sesotho is the native language in Lesotho that most of our learners have grown up speaking from their early childhood. It is therefore the medium of communication that our learners are most familiar with. We believe that the mother tongue taught at our school makes comprehension, understanding and learning easier.

Science Department

Mathematics

Mathematics is a fundamental part of human thought and logic, and integral to attempts at understanding the world and ourselves. The subject provides an effective way of building mental discipline and encourages logical reasoning and mental rigor of our learners. In addition, mathematical knowledge plays a crucial role in understanding the contents of other school subjects. Therefore, we believe that studying mathematics not only will develop more engineers and scientists but also produce more citizens who can learn and think creatively and critically, no matter their career fields.

Physcial Science

Every subject is as important as any other. Physical Science exposes a young person to new knowledge and concepts that they might never have acquired otherwise. Physical science inspires our learners towards a career in physical science that would lead to the discovery of some new and important scientific concepts or laws. It is important because it provides knowledge that heretofore would not have been known to the student.

Chemistry

Chemistry education plays an important role in enhancing the quality of teaching, research and development as well as ensuring that students are equipped with good knowledge to produce intensive good and services to mean human needs for food, health care products and other materials aimed at improving the quality of life.

Biology

Biology as a natural science which is all about the environment, human life, plant cell, animals, its functions, evolutions and all other living organisms around us. Our main view of teaching biology at Sacred Heart High School is to bring about self-awareness of our learners. This makes biology the most interesting subject which is all about life, its composition, development and about nature, environment, experiments, inventions and lot more.
